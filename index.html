FAQs

Answers that help you book with confidence.

What makes Clean Surface different from other pressure washing companies?
Steve uses soft washing for delicate exteriors and 240-degree hot-water steam for grease, gum, and stubborn stains. The method changes based on the surface instead of treating everything like concrete. You also deal with Steve directly — not a dispatch center.
Do you really guarantee driveway cleaning?
Yes. When Steve recommends a specific treatment for a driveway and the surface does not show noticeable improvement, the job is covered by a written money-back guarantee. Terms are confirmed on the quote.
Will I talk to a real person when I reach out?
Yes. Steve handles quoting and communication directly. Call or text (480) 298-3416 and you reach someone who knows the services, can explain what the job needs, and will get it scheduled.
Is soft washing safe for my stucco?
Absolutely. Soft washing uses low pressure and biodegradable detergents specifically designed for stucco and painted surfaces. It's actually safer and more effective than high-pressure washing for these materials — and it's what Steve uses on Arizona homes.
How far in advance do I need to book?
Turnaround is usually 2–5 days for most residential jobs. Commercial and larger projects may vary. Call Steve directly and he'll give you an honest timeline upfront.
