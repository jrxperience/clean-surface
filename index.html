Surface-specific work instead of guesswork

Steve uses low-pressure soft washing on stucco, painted exteriors, and delicate finishes, then switches to higher heat and more aggressive concrete methods when the job calls for it. That matters in Phoenix because dusty stucco, sun-baked concrete, gum, oil, and hard water all behave differently.

  • Soft washing for stucco, painted exteriors, and other delicate finishes

What makes Clean Surface different from a basic pressure washing company?

Steve combines soft washing for delicate exteriors with 240-degree hot-water steam for grease, gum, and stubborn stains. The method changes based on the surface instead of treating everything like concrete.

Do you really guarantee driveway cleaning?

Yes. When Steve recommends a specific treatment for a driveway and the surface does not show noticeable improvement, the job is covered by a written money-back guarantee. Terms are confirmed on the quote.

Will I be talking to a real person when I request a quote?

Yes. Steve handles quoting and communication directly. Call or text (480) 298-3416 and you reach someone who knows the services, can explain what the job needs, and will get it scheduled.
