What is the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?

Pressure washing is better for harder surfaces like concrete, while soft washing uses lower pressure and cleaning solutions chosen for more delicate finishes like stucco and painted exteriors.

When do you use hot-water steam cleaning?

Hot-water steam is typically the stronger option for grease, gum, oil, and heavier buildup that cold water alone does not break down as effectively.

Can you improve oil stains, rust marks, and hard-water buildup?

Often, yes. Those stains usually need the right pretreatment and surface-specific method. Results depend on how long the stain has been there and how deeply it has set.

How often should a home be professionally cleaned in Arizona?

Many homes do well with annual house washing, while driveways, patios, pool decks, storefronts, and customer-facing concrete often benefit from more frequent service.

Do I need to be home for exterior cleaning?

Not always. Exterior-only work can often be handled as long as access, water, and any gate or entry requirements are clear ahead of time.

Do you clean windows too?

Yes. Clean Surface offers residential and commercial window cleaning that can include the glass, frames, tracks, sills, and screens depending on the job.

How often should windows be cleaned in the Phoenix area?

Many homes schedule window cleaning two or three times a year. Storefronts and commercial properties often need more regular glass maintenance.

Can multiple services be bundled into one visit?

Yes. It is common to bundle driveway cleaning, house washing, patio or pool deck cleaning, and window cleaning so the full property presentation improves together.

What cities do you serve?

Clean Surface serves Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Gilbert, Chandler, Tempe, Glendale, Peoria, and Paradise Valley, with service built around residential, commercial, and HOA properties.

What is the best way to request a quote?

Call or text with the city, property type, surfaces to clean, the kind of buildup involved, and any scheduling deadline. That makes it much easier to recommend the right service.
