How often should windows be professionally cleaned in Arizona?

Many homes do well with service two or three times a year, while storefronts and customer-facing properties often need more frequent cleaning to stay sharp.

Can tracks, frames, screens, and sills be cleaned too?

Yes. Clean Surface can clean the full window detail so the finished result feels complete instead of stopping at the glass.

Is window cleaning safe for treated or delicate glass?

Yes. The method is matched to the glass and surrounding materials, with extra care around tinted, coated, or more delicate surfaces.
