Common questions

Driveway cleaning FAQs.

Do you really offer a driveway money-back guarantee?

Yes. When Steve recommends a specific treatment for a driveway and the surface does not show noticeable improvement, the job is covered by a written money-back guarantee. Final terms are confirmed on the quote.

Can old driveway stains be removed completely?

Sometimes, but not always. Clean Surface keeps expectations honest because concrete age, porosity, and stain type matter. The goal is noticeable improvement, not unrealistic promises.

When does hot-water steam help on driveway jobs?

Hot-water steam is especially useful for oil, grease, gum, and other stubborn buildup that cold-water washing struggles to break down.
