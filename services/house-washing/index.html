House washing service

House washing and soft washing for stucco homes, painted exteriors, and delicate finishes.

House washing is one of the fastest ways to freshen up a dusty Arizona home. Clean Surface uses the right mix of soft washing and surface-safe cleaning to brighten stucco, paint, trim, and other delicate finishes.

Soft washThe right method for stucco and paint

High pressure on stucco or painted walls can damage finish, dislodge caulk, and force water into gaps. Soft washing cleans effectively without those risks using low pressure and biodegradable detergents.

Common questions

House washing FAQs.

Is soft washing safer than regular pressure washing for stucco?

Yes. Soft washing is the better method for stucco, painted walls, trim, and siding because it avoids the pressure levels that can crack finishes, push water into gaps, or strip paint. The cleaning work is done by the detergent, not the force of the water.

What does house washing cost in Phoenix?

Most house washing jobs in the Phoenix area range from $200 to $500 depending on the home size, how much buildup is present, and whether additional surfaces are included. Steve can give a firm quote once he knows the property.

How often should a house be washed in Arizona?

Most Phoenix-area homes benefit from annual professional exterior cleaning to remove dust, pollution, and biological growth. Homes near construction zones, with heavy irrigation overspray, or in dusty corridors may need service more often.
