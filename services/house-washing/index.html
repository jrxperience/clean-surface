Is soft washing safer than regular pressure washing for stucco?

Yes. In many cases, soft washing is the better method for stucco and painted exterior surfaces because it avoids using excessive pressure.

What kinds of homes are a good fit for house washing?

Stucco homes, painted exteriors, trim-heavy facades, and properties that collect dust and streaking are all good candidates for soft washing and exterior house washing.

How often should a house be washed in Arizona?

Most homes benefit from professional exterior cleaning about once a year, though some properties need more frequent service depending on exposure, traffic, and nearby dust.
