How It Works

Surface-specific cleaning, not a one-size-fits-all wash.

The right method depends on what the surface is made of and what kind of buildup is on it. Clean Surface selects the approach based on those two things — not a default setting.

Soft Washing

For homes and delicate finishes

Stucco, painted exteriors, trim, and wood surfaces need lower pressure and the right detergent. High pressure damages finish, dislodges caulk, and forces water into gaps. Soft washing cleans effectively without those risks.

  • Low-pressure with biodegradable detergents
  • Safe for stucco, paint, trim, and fascia
  • Removes dust, mold, hard-water spots, and algae
Hot-Water Steam & Pressure

For concrete and commercial

Concrete, commercial walkways, and grease-heavy surfaces need more than a cold rinse. Clean Surface uses standard pressure for everyday concrete and 240° hot-water steam for oil stains, gum, and heavy buildup.

  • 240°F steam for grease, gum, and oil
  • Pretreatment for oil stains and heavy rust
  • Written money-back guarantee on driveway work
