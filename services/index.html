How it works

Surface-specific cleaning instead of a one-size-fits-all wash.

The right method depends on what the surface is made of and what kind of buildup is on it. Clean Surface selects the cleaning approach based on those two things, not a default setting.

Soft washing for homes and delicate finishes

Stucco, painted exteriors, trim, and wood surfaces need lower pressure and the right detergent. High pressure damages finish, dislodges caulk, and forces water into gaps. Soft washing cleans effectively without those risks.

  • Low-pressure application with biodegradable detergents
  • Safe for stucco, painted surfaces, trim, and fascia
  • Removes dust, mold, hard-water spots, and algae
  • Suitable for most Arizona residential exteriors
Hot-water steam and pressure for concrete and commercial

Concrete, commercial walkways, and grease-heavy surfaces need more than a cold rinse. Clean Surface uses standard pressure washing for everyday concrete and 240-degree hot-water steam for oil stains, gum, and heavy buildup that cold water alone does not break down.

  • Hot-water steam at 240°F for grease, gum, and oil
  • Higher-pressure concrete cleaning for driveways and walkways
  • Pretreatment for oil stains and heavy rust marks
  • Written money-back guarantee on driveway work
