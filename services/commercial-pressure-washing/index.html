Common questions

Commercial pressure washing FAQs.

What kinds of commercial properties do you clean?

Storefronts, retail centers, office building entries, parking areas, sidewalks, dumpster pads, awnings, and other customer-facing or high-traffic surfaces. For properties with graffiti, specialized removal techniques are available that eliminate the vandalism without damaging the underlying surface.

How often should a commercial property be cleaned in Phoenix?

Most commercial properties benefit from quarterly pressure washing given the desert dust and monsoon conditions. High-traffic locations like food service entries, parking structures, and retail walkways may need more frequent service to stay presentable and sanitary.

Can you schedule around business hours?

Yes. Clean Surface offers evening and weekend scheduling so the work can be handled during your slowest hours without disrupting operations or customers. Phased cleaning is available for larger properties that need to stay partly open during service.
