How often should a Phoenix home be pressure washed?

Most homes around Phoenix benefit from professional exterior cleaning about once a year. Driveways and commercial surfaces often need more frequent service because of dust, oil, and sun exposure.

Is soft washing safe for stucco and painted surfaces?

Yes. Soft washing uses lower pressure and cleaners chosen for the material, which makes it a better fit for stucco, painted exteriors, and trim.

Can pressure washing remove Arizona driveway stains?

Driveway cleaning can make a major improvement on oil spots, tire marks, dust, and hard-water residue. Older stains may lighten rather than fully disappear.
