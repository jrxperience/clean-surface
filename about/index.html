About Clean Surface

Steve Tersakian built Clean Surface to run a family-owned company that keeps exterior cleaning professional, clear, and worth it.

Clean Surface Pressure Washing is based in Phoenix and run as a family-owned business with Steve leading day-to-day quoting, customer communication, and field standards. The company handles the surfaces people notice first: dusty stucco, stained driveways, tired patios, storefront glass, commercial walkways, and concrete that can make a property look worn before anyone even walks inside.

How Clean Surface approaches the work

Steve’s approach starts with an honest assessment: inspect the surface, explain what is realistic, select the right crew and method, use the correct detergents and pressure for the material, and finish with a walkthrough. That standard applies whether the job is a Scottsdale paver patio, a Phoenix commercial dumpster pad, or a Mesa driveway with years of oil staining.

  • Free quote with a site assessment for larger jobs
  • Crew arrives in the blue CSPW truck, on time and prepared
  • Final walkthrough before the team leaves to confirm the standard was met
  • 7-day Satisfaction Promise in writing on every quote
Practices

Eco-responsible cleaning in an Arizona desert.

Clean Surface uses biodegradable, phosphate-free detergents that meet Arizona Department of Environmental Quality guidelines. On hard surfaces, the crew deploys portable berms and a vacuum-reclaim system that captures wash water so nothing harmful reaches storm drains. Water usage is minimized through high-efficiency pump equipment that delivers a thorough clean with far less flow than a standard garden hose.
