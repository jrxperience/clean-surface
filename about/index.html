Our Approach

The right method for the surface. Every time.

Steve's approach starts with an honest assessment — inspect the surface, explain what's realistic, select the right crew and method, use the correct detergents and pressure for the material, and finish with a walkthrough. That standard applies whether the job is a Scottsdale paver patio, a Phoenix commercial dumpster pad, or a Mesa driveway with years of oil staining.

  • Soft washing for stucco, painted exteriors, and delicate finishes
  • 240°F hot-water steam for grease, gum, oil, and stubborn buildup
  • Clear walk-throughs before and after — no surprise invoices
  • 7-day Satisfaction Promise in writing on every quote
  • Direct line to Steve when you need answers or scheduling help
Eco Responsibility

Clean practices in an Arizona desert.

Clean Surface uses biodegradable, phosphate-free detergents that meet Arizona Department of Environmental Quality guidelines. On hard surfaces, the crew deploys portable berms and a vacuum-reclaim system that captures wash water so nothing harmful reaches storm drains. Water usage is minimized through high-efficiency pump equipment that delivers a thorough clean with far less flow than a standard garden hose.
