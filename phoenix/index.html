How often should Phoenix homes be pressure washed?

Most homes benefit from annual cleaning, while driveways, patios, and commercial surfaces often need more frequent maintenance.

Is soft washing better for Phoenix stucco?

Yes. Soft washing is usually the safer choice for stucco and painted surfaces because it cleans effectively without using too much pressure.

Can you improve hard-water and oil staining?

Those stains can often be improved significantly, especially when the right pretreatment and hot-water cleaning methods are used.
