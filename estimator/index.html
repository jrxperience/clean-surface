Pricing Factors

What affects the final number.

  • Surface type — Stucco, pavers, and concrete all have different methods and costs
  • Total area — More square footage means more time and materials
  • Buildup severity — Heavy oil, rust, or grease may need pretreatment or steam
  • Access — Gated properties, high rooflines, or tight spaces affect crew setup
  • Bundling — Combining services in one visit often reduces the per-service cost
